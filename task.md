## Task

Build a small marketplace with features:

- **Product List**
    - Pagination (page/limit) with infinite scroll or “Load more”.
    - **Search** and category **filter**
    - **Sorting** (e.g., price asc/desc).
- **Product Details Screen**
    - Show fields, image, rating, stock.
    - “Add to cart” with quantity.
- **Cart**
    - Add/remove/update quantity.
- **Place Order** with mock requests

Implement frontend only. Mock requests by asynchronously returning the data from a promise after a short delay. Mock all data coming from backend.

Implement the logic considering there are thousands of different products in the store.
